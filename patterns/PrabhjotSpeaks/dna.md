# Prabhjot Speaks: THE NEWS-TEMPO STORYTELLER 🚨💼

**Creator Category:** News & Geopolitical Analysis
**Language Mix:** High-Tempo Hinglish (99% Hindi with News Terminology)
**Pacing (WPS):** 3.79 (Extremely Fast, Authoritative)

---

## 🧬 Linguistic DNA (Tells & Tics)

1.  **The "News Bulletin" Hook:**
    - Always starts with an urgent summary.
    - **Signature:** "इंडिया में आज की सारी बड़ी खबरें..." (All the major news in India today...) or "अगले 3..." (In the next 3 [minutes/seconds]...).
2.  **Authoritative "Darasal" & "Aankde":**
    - Uses formal transitions to cite data or "the reality".
    - **Signature:** "Darasal..." (Actually...), "Aankde batate hain..." (Data shows...).
3.  **High-Energy Adjectives:**
    - Uses words that suggest shock or finality: "Bhari vriddhi" (Huge increase), "Shatter", "Blueprint", "Tectonic shift".
4.  **Short, Punchy Sentences:**
    - Due to the high WPS (3.79), sentences are brief. He packs multiple facts into a 60-second window.

---

## 🏗️ Structural Logic

1.  **Hook (0-5s):** Urgent news headline + "Breaking News" vibe.
2.  **Context (5-20s):** Rapid-fire explanation of the "what" and "where".
3.  **The Twist/Impact (20-45s):** Why this matters to India or the global map.
4.  **Evidence (45-55s):** Specific numbers (₹, $, %, millions).
5.  **Closing (55-60s):** Standardized CTA: "Current affairs ke liye mujhe follow zaroor karna." (Make sure to follow me for current affairs).

---

## 🎨 Visual System

- **Background:** Dynamic newsroom or overlay of high-quality news footage.
- **Text:** Bold red/white "BREAKING" banners.
- **Transitions:** Fast wipes or zooms.

---

## 🧪 SCRIPT_RULES (Prabhjot-Specific)

- **WPS Target:** 3.7 - 3.9 (Never drop below 3.5).
- **Hook Rule:** MUST start with "India mein aaj ki..." or "Breaking News!".
- **Language Rule:** Use formal Hindi words like 'Shatter', 'Monopoly', 'Sovereignty' as they add to the "News" feel.
- **Closing Rule:** ALWAYS use the "Follow zaroor karna" CTA.
