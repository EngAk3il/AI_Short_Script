# Strategy Enrichment Rules (MANDATORY FOR HYBRID SCRIPTS)

> This document defines how to blend **Creator DNA** (Voice/Tone) with **Strategy DNA** (Virality/Retention).
> The goal is to keep the creator's soul while upgrading the video's performance.

---

## 1. The Strategy-First Hook (The Ethan Shaw / TubeBuddy Rule)

The hook is the most critical part of the enrichment.

- **Rule:** The Strategy Hook (Type A/B/C from the Strategy DNA) **ALWAYS** overrides the regular Creator Hook.
- **Implementation:** If you are enriching with `Ethan Shaw`, you MUST start with a "Narrative Tension" hook, even if the creator (like `Shivanshu`) usually starts with a "Fact-first" hook.
- **Blending:** Use the strategy's *structure* but the creator's *language*.
    - *Example (Shivanshu + Ethan Shaw):* Instead of "Ratan Tata was born in 1937," use "Look, it was 1937 and Ratan Tata had a problem most people ignore." (Look = Ethan, 1937 = Shivanshu).

---

## 2. Retention Interruption (The TubeBuddy Rule)

Creators often ramble. Strategy experts don't.

- **Rule:** Every 10 seconds (or 3-4 lines), you MUST insert a **Strategy Reset**.
- **Implementation:** Use the strategy's "Pattern Interrupt" guidelines.
    - *TubeBuddy Style:* "Wait... there's one thing most people miss."
    - *Ethan Shaw Style:* "But then, everything changed."
- **Visuals:** Add specific visual cues (Fast Zoom, Sound Effect, Text Overlay) that the Strategy expert would use, even if the creator's DNA doesn't mention them.

---

## 3. The Value-First Mechanism (The Hormozi / Aevy Rule)

Don't just give information; explain the **unfair advantage**.

- **Rule:** If enriching with `Hormozi`, simplify all logic until a 10-year-old could understand the value.
- **Blending:** Use the creator's niche (e.g., Zomato origin) but use Hormozi's "Radical Transparency" to reveal *exactly* how they made their first $100.

---

## 4. The Infinite Loop (The Retention Special)

To increase re-watches, every enriched script must loop.

- **Rule:** The last line of the script MUST be a non-final conclusion that grammatically or logically leads back to the first line.
- **Implementation:**
    - *Last line:* "...and that's the secret behind..."
    - *First line:* "...the Billion Dollar Menu Card."

---

## 5. The Narrative Matrix (V3 Dynamic Skeletons)

To prevent "Static Generation," rotate these 4 story arcs for every script. Do NOT use the same skeleton twice in a row for the same creator.

| Skeleton | Structural Flow | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Flashback** | [Shock Result] → [Flashback to Origin] → [The Struggle] → [Resolution] | Brand/Origin Stories |
| **The Cumulative** | [Simple Observation] → ["But it's deeper"] → [Mechanism] → [Big Outcome] | Educational/Tech |
| **The Counter-Intuitive** | [Common Logic] → ["Why it's wrong"] → [The Real Truth] → [The Proof] | Psychology/Market Analysis |
| **The Mystery Loop** | [The Riddle] → [Clue 1] → [Clue 2] → [The Reveal] → [Loop to Riddle] | High-Retention/Growth |

---

## 6. Dynamic Pacing (The WPS Map)

Avoid steady-speed narration. Vary the **Words Per Second (WPS)** based on the emotional segment:

- **Hook (0-7s):** Fast (3.8+ WPS). Aggressive, high-energy.
- **Story/Context:** Moderate (3.2 - 3.5 WPS). Narrative flow.
- **Mechanism/Value:** Slow (2.8 - 3.1 WPS). Allow the concepts to breathe.
- **CTA/Loop:** Fast (3.5+ WPS). Momentum for the re-watch.

## 8. The Anti-Static Protocol (MANDATORY)

To prevent scripts from becoming robotic or formulaic ("static"), every generation must follow these rules:

### A. Context-First Selection
*   **Rule:** Do NOT force a strategy component if the topic does not support it. 
*   **Example:** If enriching with **Alex Hormozi**, but the topic is purely historical with no business stakes, do NOT use "Price Anchoring." Instead, use his "Radical Transparency" or "Foundation" logic.

### B. Structural Shuffling (The Timing Rule)
*   **Rule:** Do NOT place Strategy Resets (Pattern Interrupts) at the same intervals every time.
*   **Implementation:** Vary the placement:
    -   Script 1: Reset at 7s, 22s, 45s.
    -   Script 2: Reset at 12s, 30s.
    -   **Avoid the "Every 10 Seconds" trap.**

### C. The "Creator Soul" Filter (The Dominance Rule)
*   **Rule:** The Creator's DNA (Voice/Tone) is the **Master**. The Strategy is the **Slave**.
*   **Implementation:** If the strategy makes **Shivanshu** sound too aggressive (like **Hormozi**), soften the strategy language to fit the "Museum Guide" tone. 
*   **Check:** Would the creator actually say this word? If no, rewrite the strategy logic into the creator's vocabulary.

### D. Narrative Arc Variation
*   **Rule:** Rotate the story skeleton for every script. Never use the same flow twice in a row:
    -   **Flow 1:** Flashback (End → Beginning → Resolution).
    -   **Flow 2:** Chronological (Beginning → Middle → End).
    -   **Flow 3:** Mystery (The Riddle → The Investigation → The Reveal).
