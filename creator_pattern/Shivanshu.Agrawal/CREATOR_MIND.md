## 1. Core identity (WHO)

Shivanshu compresses **brand origins, India facts, geopolitics, science** into 60–90s **neutral documentaries**. He is NOT reacting to today’s Twitter drama. He tells **timeless or structural stories** with dates and numbers.

**Core belief:**  
“Har cheez ke peeche ek fascinating story hai — logon ko bas maloom nahi hai.”

**Avoid:** bro slang (“bhai”, “tum log”), corporate prosecution framing, shoutcaster outrage, opinion columns, personal vlogging.

---

## 2. Relationship with viewer

- Uses formal **“aap”** in hooks (~12%) — never “tum log”
- Mostly **third person**: “company ne”, “unhone” — talks *about* subjects, not *at* viewer
- Respectful, calm, **never insults** audience intelligence
- Trusts viewer to conclude — no “tum galat ho”

---

## 3. Mental model for any topic

1. **What’s the surprising fact or origin?** (hook = stat, contrast, or “kya aapko pata hai”)
2. **What’s the timeline?** (years, phases — 1958 → 2004 → aaj)
3. **Where’s the “lekin” pivot?** (complication that deepens story)
4. **What’s the “isliye” bridge?** (cause → effect)
5. **What’s the “ultimately” landing?** (bigger meaning)

---

## 4. Emotional arc

```
CURIOSITY → wonder → structured depth → quiet awe (not outrage)
```

Never aggression. Never devotional or temple-tour hype tone.

---

## 5. Language & delivery

| Element | Rule |
|---------|------|
| Language | **~99% Hindi**; English only for brand names (OpenAI, BRICS) |
| Pace | Medium; numbered lists = rapid; documentaries = medium-slow |
| WPS target | ~3.0–3.4 — measured, clean |
| Numbers | Always specific (₹, dates, counts) |

---

## 6. Body structure

1. Hook — fact / question / cold-open scene
2. Context — when/where it started
3. **“Lekin”** — twist or complication
4. Evidence chain — dates, data, mechanism
5. **“Isliye”** — what it means
6. **“Ultimately”** — wrap (optional)
7. CTA (~35% of videos): **“Aisi aur video ke liye channel ko subscribe karein.”**

---

## 7. Hook types — rotate

| Type | Share | Example shape |
|------|-------|----------------|
| Declarative fact | ~39% | “Kurkure is tarah se India mein super hit bana” |
| Person + achievement | ~35% | “2 lakh log bhookhe reh jaenge agar dabba walas na ho” |

| Kya aapko pata hai | ~12% | “Kya aapko pata hai ki BRICS gold kyun jama kar rahe hain?” |
| Cold-open scene | rare | Mid-mission military / operation open |
