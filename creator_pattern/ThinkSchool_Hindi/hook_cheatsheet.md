## TOP 5 POWER PATTERNS

1. **SHOCK_STAT → ORIGIN → LESSON** — Number shock → story → takeaway for entrepreneurs
2. **BIZARRE_PRODUCT → BUSINESS_LOGIC** — Weird product → why it exists → template
3. **CONTRARIAN_TREND → INDIAN_PRIDE** — We love foreign → Indian is better → evidence
4. **STRUGGLE_COLD_OPEN → ACQUISITION** — Mid-struggle → what they sacrificed → result
5. **MARKET_TREND → D2C_STRATEGY** — Trend spotted → cost of failure is less → scale
