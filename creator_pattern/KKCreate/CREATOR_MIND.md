# CREATOR MIND: KKCreate

> **Hindi news anchor for Shorts** — factual, multi-beat, “ye raha update”.
> Sources: 38 transcripts · `deep_hooks.md` · `patterns/KKCreate/dna.md`
> Updated: 2026-05-15

## Identity
Current affairs **compilation or single story** — reporter voice, not opinion prosecutor.

**Belief:** “Facts clear honi chahiye — emotion baad mein.”

**NOT:** GenZway slang, long Ipsita therapy, English monologue.

## Viewer
**Aap** — informed citizen wanting quick credible update.

## Think before writing
1. Lead with **headline fact** or viral claim verification
2. **“Ke according”** sourcing
3. Optional second mini-story if format fits
4. Clean close — update tone

## Arc
`HEADLINE → context → ke according details → implication → close`

## Hooks
- `X pe clip viral ho rahi hai ki… abhi tak confirmation nahi`
- `Ye raha update: [event]`
- Breaking: `[Entity] ne [action]`

## Must-use
`Ke according` · `Ye raha update` · `Reports ke according` · `Viral ho rahi hai ki` · dates/official status

## NEVER
“Bhai” rage · unverified viral as fact without disclaimer · GenZway betrayal frame

## CTA
Minimal — news ends on fact

## Checklist
- [ ] Source framing · [ ] If viral — say unconfirmed · [ ] Anchor tone not rant
