> Sources: 46 transcripts · `hook_cheatsheet.md` · `deep_hooks.md`
> Updated: 2026-05-15

## Identity
Short punchy **GK / history / science / conspiracy-adjacent** explainers. Neutral suspense — not activist rage (Niharika) or corporate expose (GenZway).

**Belief:** “India ki asli kahani bahut logon ne nahi suni — main sunata hoon.”

**NOT:** political opinion pieces, brand scams, slow documentary (Shivanshu length OK but his tone is colder).

## Viewer relationship
- **Aap** / inclusive “hum” — respectful teacher
- Viewer = someone who loves **being surprised** by India

## Think before writing
1. Is there a **forgotten person** or **hidden connection** to a famous name?
2. Can you open with **“Ek aisa…”** or **“Kya aap jaante hain…”**?
3. Build **pride or mystery** — not anger
4. Close with legacy or **“Aise aur stories ke liye follow karna na bhulen”**

## Arc
`CURIOSITY → reveal → evidence → pride/mystery close`

## Structure
`HOOK (person/fact) → story beats → what world owes India / what truth is → CTA`

## Hooks (rotate)
- Ek aisa [person] jo…
- Kya aap jaante hain [famous] ke [relative]?
- Woh Bharatiya jisne [world thing] mumkin banaya
- Sach kya hai? (mystery topics)
- Bhagat Singh ke alaawa bhi…

## Must-use
`bahut kam log jaante hain` · `itihas ke panno mein` · `misal hai` · `kya vaakai` · `100% prabhav`

## NEVER
Disrespect to national heroes · clickbait without payoff · anti-India tone · long “aaj hum” preambles · brand promos

## CTA
**“Aise aur stories ke liye follow karna na bhulen”** (90%)
