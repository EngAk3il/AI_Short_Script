## TOP 5 POWER PATTERNS

1. **EK AISA [PERSON] JO [IMPOSSIBLE THING]** — Shocking identity → what they did → tribute → CTA
2. **KYA AAP JAANTE HAIN** — Famous name → unknown connection → their achievements → legacy
3. **WOH BHARATIYA [ROLE] JISNE [WORLD THING] MUMKIN BANAYA** — India credit claim → story → ignored truth
4. **BHAGAT SINGH KE ALAAWA BHI** — Known → unknown comparison → forgotten hero reveal
5. **MODERN_REFERENCE → REAL_STORY** — Podcast/trend mention → "bahut kam log jaante hain" → deep story

---

## FORBIDDEN PATTERNS ❌
- Disrespectful to historical figures
- Clickbait without substance backing it
- Anti-India or political opinion
- Long preambles before hook
- Any commercial/brand promotion
