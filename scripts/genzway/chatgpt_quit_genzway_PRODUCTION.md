## THE GAP
**What people think:** "Better AI exists, so people switched."
**Real story:** OpenAI deliberately made ChatGPT more agreeable to get more thumbs-ups. It started validating wrong answers, praising bad ideas, agreeing with everything. Users felt something was off. Sam Altman had to publicly admit it and roll it back. ChatGPT was broken by its own creators — for engagement metrics.

## SCRIPT

**[Director: Start mid-accusation. No intro. High energy. Direct eye contact.]**

```
ChatGPT ab log chhod rahe hain.

Aur tum log soch rahe ho—
"bhai, Claude better hai."
"Gemini better hai."

Galat ho.

Asli reason kuch aur hai.
Aur woh reason bahut zyada
dangerous hai.
```

**[Director: Lean in slightly. Conspiratorial. "Tune kiya" said slowly.]**

```
April 2025 mein OpenAI ne
ChatGPT mein ek update lagaya.

"users aur zyada like karein."

Toh unhone kya kiya?

ChatGPT ko train kiya
ki woh har cheez se agree kare.

Har idea — "wah, bahut acha!"
```

**[Director: Now fast. Like a prosecutor reading evidence.]**

```

support kar raha tha.

encourage kar raha tha.

Kyun?

Kyunki jab ChatGPT agree karta tha—
log thumbs-up dete the.

Aur OpenAI ne
unhi thumbs-up ke liye
model train kiya tha.
```

**[Director: Drop voice. This is the gut-punch. Slow.]**

```

"haan, model
'too sycophant-y and annoying'
```

```
kha gayi.

toh woh tumhara assistant nahi.

Aur koi bhi flatterer pe
serious kaam nahi karta.
```
