## WHY THIS SCRIPT WORKS (Read Before Recording)

Every second of a Short has ONE job:
- **0–3s** → Stop the thumb. Create a question or betrayal in viewer's head.
- **3–15s** → Reward with context. Don't answer yet. Build tension.
- **15–40s** → Evidence stack. Each line adds weight.
- **40–60s** → The twist / real villain revealed.
- **60–75s** → Payoff + close. Viewer feels smart for watching.

## FULL SCRIPT WITH TIMESTAMPS + DIRECTOR NOTES

---

### 🔴 HOOK — 0:00 to 0:07
**[Director: HIGH ENERGY. Direct eye contact. No intro. No "hey guys". Start mid-thought like viewer walked in late.]**

```
RCB bik gayi.
₹16,000 crore mein.

Aur tum log soch rahe ho—
"wah, kitna bada deal."

Bhai.
Tum game samajhe hi nahi.
```

**Why it works:** "Tum game samajhe hi nahi" is a direct ego challenge. Viewer CANNOT scroll away — they have to prove they understand.

---

### 🟡 CONTEXT BUILD — 0:07 to 0:22
**[Director: Slightly slower. Deliberate. Each buyer name = one pause. Let it sink.]**

```
Ye companies ne cricket team nahi kharidi.

Aditya Birla.         [pause]
Times of India.       [pause]
Blackstone.           [pause]
Bolt Ventures.        [pause]

Inhe cricket se koi matlab nahi.

Inhe ek cheez chahiye thi—
tum.

Tumhara attention.
Tumhari emotions.
Tumhari loyalty.
```

**Why it works:** "Inhe ek cheez chahiye thi — tum." shifts from corporate to personal. Viewer is now the subject of the video, not RCB.

---

### 🟠 EVIDENCE STACK — 0:22 to 0:42
**[Director: Fast, staccato delivery. Numbers like facts in a court case. No emotion — just evidence.]**

```
RCB ka merchandise—
2015 mein ₹10 crore tha.

2025 mein?
₹150 crore ka ecosystem.

PUMA ne confirm kiya—
trophy ke baad jersey sales mein
300% spike aaya.

Sirf ek trophy se.
Sirf ek season mein.

45 million followers.
Instagram par IPL ki
sabse followed team.

Ye sirf cricket nahi—
ye ek attention machine hai.
```

**Why it works:** Numbers stack like evidence. Viewer is building a mental case alongside you. Each stat confirms what they already suspected.

---

### 🔵 THE TWIST / REAL VILLAIN — 0:42 to 1:00
**[Director: Slow down. Conspiratorial tone. Almost like you're telling a secret. Lean in.]**

```
Ab darkest part suno.

Bechne wala kaun tha?
Diageo.

Official line—
"core business pe focus karna hai."

Corporate language mein translate karo—
"yaar, isse zyada mehengi
yeh kabhi nahi hogi.
Abhi becho."

RCB champion thi.
Fan emotion 17 saal mein sabse high.
Valuation peak.

Tab becha.

Tum log trophy celebrate kar rahe the.
Investors exit kar rahe the.
```

**Why it works:** "Tum log trophy celebrate kar rahe the. Investors exit kar rahe the." — this is the gut-punch line. Creates betrayal feeling without being explicitly accusatory.

---

### 🟢 PAYOFF / CLOSE — 1:00 to 1:15
**[Director: SLOW. Almost cold. Like a verdict being read. Direct camera. No smile.]**

```
Yahi fark hota hai—
ordinary fans aur
billion-dollar investors ke beech.

Game stadium mein nahi chal raha.
Game tumhare attention ke andar
chal raha hai.

Aur jab tak tum yeh nahi samjhoge—
tum hamesha woh rahouge
jinka attention bika ja raha hai.
```

**Why it works:** Last line is confrontational but true. It doesn't let viewer off the hook. They either share it (to prove they get it) or think about it.

## SCRIPT STRUCTURE MAP

```
HOOK (0-7s)     → EGO CHALLENGE — "tum samjhe hi nahi"
CONTEXT (7-22s) → PERSONAL STAKES — "inhe chahiye thi TUM"
EVIDENCE (22-42s) → NUMBER STACK — viewers build case
TWIST (42-60s)  → VILLAIN REVEAL — Diageo decoded
PAYOFF (60-75s) → GUT PUNCH — "tum hamesha woh rahouge"
```

## DELIVERY RULES FOR THIS SCRIPT

| Section | Pace | Tone | Body Language |
|---------|------|------|---------------|
| Hook (0-7s) | Fast | Accusatory | Direct eye, no smile |
| Context (7-22s) | Medium | Cold fact | Each name = pause |
| Evidence (22-42s) | Fast staccato | Court prosecutor | Numbers confidently |
| Twist (42-60s) | Slow | Conspiratorial | Lean in slightly |
| Payoff (60-75s) | Slowest | Verdict | Still, direct |

---

## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

- ❌ Don't start with "hey guys aaj hum baat karte hain"
- ❌ Don't explain what you're about to say — just say it
- ❌ Don't smile during the evidence stack — you're a prosecutor
- ❌ Don't rush the twist section — that's where viewers decide to share
- ❌ Don't add extra "isliye" filler lines — every word earns its place
