# RCB ₹16,000 Crore — Kya Aapko Pata Hai? | SHIVANSHU AGRAWAL

---

Kya aapko pata hai ki
RCB ₹16,000 crore mein biki—

aur isko kharidne waali
char companies mein se kisi ek ka bhi
cricket se seedha koi kaam nahi tha?

---

Numbers pehle samjhiye.

RCB ka merchandise—
2015 mein approximately ₹10 crore.
2025 tak?
₹150 crore ka growing ecosystem.

PUMA ke according—
2025 trophy ke baad
jersey sales mein 300% spike aaya.

IPL 2025—
1 billion combined viewers.
840 billion watch minutes.

---

Lekin yahan pe interesting baat yeh hai—

Aditya Birla Group ne kharida.
165 saal purana empire.
Retail. Telecom. Fashion. Sab kuch.

Missing thi ek cheez—
India ke young generation se
direct emotional connection.

RCB unke liye
sirf sports investment nahi.

Youth culture mein
entry ticket hai.

---

Times of India ne kharida.
Jinke paas pehle se
Cricbuzz aur Willow TV hai.

Ab content bhi inke.
Team bhi inki.

Ek media analyst ke according—
ye wahi strategy hai
jaise Netflix apna studio bhi own kare
aur cinema hall bhi.

---

Bolt Ventures—David Blitzer.
NBA. NFL. EPL.
Sab mein invested.

India mein kyun aaye?
Europe-America ke sports markets
mature ho chuke hain.

India abhi explode hona baaki hai.

Aur Blackstone—
sirf numbers dekhte hain.
Aur numbers clear the.

---

Bechne wala?
Diageo.

Official reason—
"core business par focus karna."

Lekin timing pe dhyan dijiye—
RCB champion thi.
Fanbase peak par tha.
Emotion peak par tha.

Isliye—
yeh ₹16,000 crore
sirf ek sports deal nahi.

Yeh ek signal hai.

Future mein sabse ameer companies
woh nahi hongi
jo sabse zyada products banayengi.

Woh hongi—
jo sabse zyada loyal audience
own karengi.

Ultimately—
RCB ne sirf trophy nahi jeeta.
Usne khud ko
ek billion-dollar emotion machine
sabit kiya.
