## THE GAP
**What people think:** The 2026 Bengal elections were a settled mandate.
**Real story:** Reports of massive "Special Intensive Revision" (SIR) fraud in the Falta constituency led to a rare, high-stakes re-poll order that could flip the final balance of power.

## SCRIPT

---

**[Director: Serious, direct tone. Start with the "Our state is burning" vibe but focused on democracy.]**

```
Aapko News channels 
Israel ya Iran ki news zarur dikha rahe honge.

Lekin kya aapko pata hai ki
apne hi desh ke ek state mein
democracy ke saath kya ho raha hai?

Election Commission ne
Falta constituency mein
re-poll ka order diya hai.
```

**[Director: Pivot. "Yeh law and order ka failure hai".]**

```
Sawal yeh nahi hai ki re-poll kyun ho raha hai.
Sawal yeh hai ki—
pehle se mandate milne ke bawajood
irregularities itni badi kaise ho gayi?

Yeh system ka failure hai.
Aur iska asar pure West Bengal ke
final results par pad sakta hai.
```

**[Director: Call to action / Question.]**

Where is our democracy?
